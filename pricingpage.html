Pricing Details

Our pricing is calculated based on distance, weight, and type of goods being transported. Below is a breakdown of how we determine costs:

Distance Weight Price Estimate
0 - 10 km Up to 5 kg ₹375
10 - 50 km 5 - 20 kg ₹1125
50+ km 20 - 50 kg ₹2250+

Pricing Formula:

Total Price = Base Fare + (Rate per km × Distance) + (Weight Factor × Weight)

Explanation of Each Component:

  • Base Fare (₹200) → A fixed charge applied to every booking.
  • Rate per km (₹10/km) → Charges applied based on the total distance between pickup and drop locations.
  • Weight Factor (₹2/kg) → Additional charge based on the weight of the goods being transported.
